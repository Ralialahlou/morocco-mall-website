Who May Enter

The Rose Boulevard Lounge is reserved for those whose relationship with Morocco Mall reflects the highest distinction.

Aksal Black Programme

Black Gold & Diamond Members

Holders of the Aksal Black Gold or Diamond card benefit from unlimited complimentary access to the Rose Boulevard Lounge throughout the day, simply upon presentation of their membership card at the Lounge entrance.

Same-Day Purchases

Rose Boulevard Clients — 5,000 MAD+

Guests who have made cumulative purchases of 5,000 MAD or more within Rose Boulevard on the same day are granted complimentary Lounge access for the remainder of the visit. Proof of purchase is required at the Lounge reception desk.

The Experience

Four Pillars of Excellence

Every moment spent in the Lounge is curated to elevate your visit to the level of absolute luxury.

Champagne & Refreshments

A curated selection of prestige champagnes, fine wines, artisanal teas and bespoke mocktails, served by a dedicated butler throughout your stay in the Lounge.

Personal Stylist on Request

A personal stylist is available upon advance reservation to guide your selections across Rose Boulevard's boutiques, offering expert curation tailored to your individual aesthetic.

Our dedicated concierge liaises directly with each maison to arrange private viewings, reserve exclusive pieces, coordinate personalization requests and manage all logistics of your purchases.

Valet Dedicated Service

Lounge guests benefit from priority valet parking, with vehicles retrieved on demand. A seamless arrival and departure experience befitting the Rose Boulevard standard of excellence.

The Space

180 m² of

Access Protocol

How to Access the Lounge

Entering the Rose Boulevard Lounge is a simple, graceful process designed to respect your time.

01

Confirm Eligibility

Ensure you hold an Aksal Black Gold or Diamond card, or have made same-day Rose Boulevard purchases of 5,000 MAD or more, receipts in hand.

02

Locate the Lounge

Proceed to Level 2, Rose Boulevard. The Lounge entrance is situated at the centre of the avenue, marked by the discreet gold signature facade and reception podium.

03

Present Your Credentials

Our host team will welcome you and verify your eligibility. Presentation of your Aksal Black card or same-day purchase receipts takes moments and is handled with full discretion.

04

Enjoy Your Retreat

You are welcomed into the Lounge and free to enjoy all services, spaces and amenities at your own pace for the duration of your visit to Morocco Mall.
